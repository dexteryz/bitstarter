Virtuoso enables you to easily collaborate on writing music online. It's frustrating to try and produce music with others online through video, notetaking apps, and audio clips. Instead, Virtuoso enables you to produce, edit, and critique songs using an online music notation-based workstation and invite others into your space to work on the same songs.

FAQ

What's the story behind Virtuoso?
It's not easy to find other musicians to collaborate with on songwriting. I had a hard time seeking them out, so I wanted to see if anyone else had the same problem.
What's the hardest thing about songwriting with someone else?
It's often very challenging to find someone who is a good match musically.
