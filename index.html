"I've tried dozens of productivity apps... but none of them help me focus."

That was the same disgruntled situation I was in before I discovered President Eisenhower's secret weapon: the urgent-importance matrix. His matrix is as efficient and tactical as he was.

Lo and behold, the Ike Method reincarnates Eisenhower's secret weapon in a targeted to-do list app that helps you focus on what's important and urgent. It's tough to keep track of all those matrices on paper, so we brought it to your iPhone.
